Data Types:

Integer (int64): 12 columns
Float (float64): 3 columns
Object (string): 5 columns


ID
Unique identifier for each individual (customer).

Gender
Gender of the individual (0 for male, 1 for female).

Own_car
Binary feature indicating whether the individual owns a car (0 for no, 1 for yes).

Own_property
Binary feature indicating whether the individual owns a property (0 for no, 1 for yes).

Work_phone
Binary feature indicating whether the individual has a work phone (0 for no, 1 for yes).

Phone
Binary feature indicating whether the individual has a phone (0 for no, 1 for yes).

Email
Binary feature indicating whether the individual has provided an email address (0 for no, 1 for yes).

Unemployed
Binary feature indicating whether the individual is unemployed (0 for no, 1 for yes).

Num_children
Number of children the individual has.

Num_family
Total number of family members.

Account_length
Length of the individual's account with a bank or financial institution.

Total_income
Total income of the individual.

Age
Age of the individual.

Years_employed
Number of years the individual has been employed.

Income_type
Type of income (e.g., employed, self-employed, etc.).

Education_type
Education level of the individual.

Family_status
Family status of the individual.

Housing_type
Type of housing the individual lives in.

Occupation_type
Type of occupation the individual is engaged in.

Target
Target variable indicating whether the individual is eligible for a credit card (0 for not eligible, 1 for eligible).
